Multimedia

HTML5 Video element

The second Assignment was optional, We had to look up some of the "enhanced" HTML5 video players available on the web.

We had to play around with the new video element to discover its functions. For example we could let the video play in a loop, or let video's play one after each other.

We could also try to make a custom videoplayer with custom buttons.

For the more experienced programmers there where options to add subtitles and audiotracks, and they could try out the new WebAudio API.
